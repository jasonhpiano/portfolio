Circera - Hemp Care

Visual Identity Design

Project Overview

Circera aspires to become a prominent name in the luxury skincare industry, emphasizing the utilization of hemp-based ingredients to craft premium, natural, and highly effective skincare products. The primary goal is to create a timeless and elegant brand identity that encapsulates the essence of Circera.

The inclusion of mockups for social media, packaging, and business cards enables a tangible representation of how the brand identity would be translated across various applications, reinforcing Circera's identity as a leading luxury skincare brand.

The design process began by gaining a deep understanding of the brand through a project brief. Subsequently, we developed design directions using moodboards. Once a clear and aligned design direction was established, we proceeded to create the logo with its variations, establish the color palette, select the typography suite, and design patterns.



Visual Direction Moodboard
This concept is inspired by the natural allure of Earth's palette with our moodboard celebrating the beauty of contrasting earth-toned colors. Elevate the essence of youthfulness through the use of elegant serif fonts and sophisticated patterns. The logotype brings a touch of sophistication while maintaining the overall natural, young, and feminine vibe, encapsulating the perfect blend of organic charm and contemporary appeal.

Craft Lab

Visual Identity Design

Project Overview

Craft Lab aims to develop a brand identity that exudes Dynamic, organic, and strong, reflecting Craft Lab's commitment to quality and environmental responsibility.

The design process began by thoroughly understanding the brand’s attributes and target audience through a detailed project brief. Subsequently, we developed visual moodboards to capture the dynamic and organic essence of Craft Lab and explored various design directions. After aligning on a clear and vibrant design direction, we moved forward with creating the logo, exploring different variations to ensure versatility. We established a rich and earthy color palette, reflecting the brand’s organic roots and dynamic energy, and selected strong and modern typography to convey the brand’s robust character. Dynamic patterns and motifs inspired by coffee beans and natural elements were created to enhance brand storytelling.



Visual Direction Moodboard
This concept draws inspiration from the robust and natural elements of coffee cultivation, with our moodboard celebrating the dynamic and earthy tones found in nature. Elevate the essence of strength and organic authenticity through the use of bold sans-serif fonts and modern, clean patterns. The logotype embodies a sense of vigor and reliability while maintaining the overall dynamic and natural vibe, encapsulating the perfect blend of strength, vitality, and environmental consciousness.

Social Media Design

Project Overview

United Sleep Apnea Services is a company that specializes in sleep home testing. They give service that treats people with sleeping issues such as sleep apnea.

The social media designs and infographics for their website purpose are to bring awarenesa to people who have sleep disorders. Aside from bringing awareness, the context also provides solutions.

The goal is to convey the emotional stress and what it takes away from someone who has sleep disorders(along with comorbidities) and shows healthiness and excitement and joy of being healthy.
